# Benchmark Results — 2026-03-03

**Machine**: Apple Silicon (arm64), macOS 24.5.0
**ilo**: release + cranelift (`cargo build --release --features cranelift`)
**Node**: v24.9.0 (V8)
**LuaJIT**: 2.1.1748459687
**Python**: CPython 3 (system)

All times are **ns per call** (lower is better). Each language runs its own warmup + iteration loop.
fib uses n=15 (ilo-interp is slow on recursive benchmarks at higher n).

## Key Takeaways

- **ilo-vm beats Python** on 8/10 benchmarks, 1.1–16x faster
- **ilo-jit wins on record-heavy workloads**: records (7x vs Python), hashmap (8x vs Python), matmul (10x vs Python) — bump arena allocation pays off
- **ilo-jit vs V8**: within 2x on records/hashmap; 10x slower on most others
- **LuaJIT and V8 are peer tier**: 1–4K ns on numeric/branching, ilo-vm is ~100x behind them there
- **strings is ilo's strongest relative showing**: ilo-jit (379K ns) beats LuaJIT (678K ns), within 1.5x of V8 (259K ns)
- **ilo-interp is ~10-25x slower than Python** — expected for a tree-walking interpreter vs CPython bytecode
